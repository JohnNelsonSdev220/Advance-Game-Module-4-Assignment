# Reflection Document

When creating the animations for my character. I decided to go with a dirty/muddy and hurt look. I wanted to do that because originally they were clean, but I thought it looked too "doughy," and I struggled to create the weight painting I imagined in my head, and although I still imagined it better, I find it a lot better where I can actually tell the difference between their head, left arm, right arm, left leg, right leg, and torso (which make up at least 5 bones) 

The way I created depth for the character was by making their legs and arms appear to be going back and forth, but in the art style, I'm really just shrinking them down and moving them closer to the torso to create the look of them gaining or losing depth.

Finally, I have 6 animations for each idle and walk, each with 200 milliseconds, making it 12000 milliseconds or 1.2 seconds each animation.
